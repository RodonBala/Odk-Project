Types of honey on our farm

Here are many types of honey on the market but we produce three types of them

Organge Blossom Honey

This clear honey is collected from hives in the orange groves of Mexico. It has a deliciously distinctive orange flavour and a light amber colour.

Mountain Honey

Mountain honey is helpful for stress, disorders of the endocrine system, cardiovascular diseases, and effectively treating diseases of the kidneys, liver, and stomach.

Clover Honey

Clover honey is high in flavanols, antioxidants that can help to regulate your blood pressure, which in turn helps to protect your heart health.
